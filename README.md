# ZamAI

ZamAI is an AI-first software organization building language technology, educational products, customer-facing AI experiences, and practical business systems—starting with solutions designed for Pashto-speaking and underserved markets.

## What we build

### ZamAI Labs
Research and platform work that powers our products.
- Pashto datasets and data pipelines
- Model training & fine-tuning
- NLP tooling and evaluation
- Reusable AI services and internal SDKs

### ZamAI Products
Customer-facing AI experiences.
- **Zeerak** — our flagship AI assistant experience
- Pashto keyboard and typing tools
- AI-powered apps and utilities that ship to real users

### ZamAI Education
Mission-driven products for learning and empowerment.
- ZamMadrasa
- EduSpeak
- Learning tools for students and educators
- Community and study support experiences

### ZamAI Systems
Practical software for organizations and institutions.
- HR, attendance, finance
- Pharmacy and inventory
- Document archiving and office automation
- Workflow digitization for real-world operations

## How we work
- **Mobile-first**: great UX on mid-tier devices
- **Feature-based architecture**: modular, maintainable codebases
- **Type-safe**: TypeScript-first where possible
- **Accessible**: accessibility is a requirement, not a bonus
- **Performance-minded**: optimize re-renders, avoid heavy client work, use virtualization where needed
- **Respectful and culturally aware**: especially in language and education products

## Repositories
We organize work across four divisions:
- `labs/` (models, datasets, pipelines)
- `products/` (end-user apps)
- `education/` (learning + community)
- `systems/` (business/institutional apps)

See the pinned repositories for the most active work.

## Contributing
We welcome collaborators, especially in:
- Pashto NLP datasets and evaluation
- Mobile UX and localization (RTL/LTR)
- Education tooling and content workflows
- Operational systems digitization

When contributing:
1. Open an issue describing the change and context.
2. Keep changes focused and well-tested.
3. Follow the repo’s architecture and conventions.
4. Include documentation updates when behavior changes.

## Security & Responsible AI
Some repos may contain model/data work. Please avoid sharing sensitive data, and follow responsible AI practices:
- do not include private or identifying user data in datasets
- document dataset sources and license constraints
- evaluate for harmful outputs and bias where applicable

## Contact
For partnerships, pilots, or collaboration, open an issue in the relevant repository or contact the maintainers listed in that repo.
